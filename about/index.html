Personal

I built Kynar Universe because I wanted a space that was entirely mine — not a template, not a portfolio, not a profile. A world. Something that exists whether anyone visits it or not.

I'm teaching myself to build things properly. HTML, CSS, JavaScript. The lab is where that happens in public. The world is where the fiction lives. The game is where they meet.

I don't have a name on this site. That's intentional. What I make is here. That's enough.
