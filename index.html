Concierge FAQ

Common inquiries regarding the Kynar Shop.

How is the access delivered?
Immediately following authorization, a secure link is dispatched to your email via Lemon Squeezy. Direct portal access is also available on the success page.
Software requirements?
Kynar assets are platform-optimized. Our organization systems typically run on Notion, while creative assets utilize Canva or Lightroom. Check specific product specs for details.
Commercial licensing?
All Kynar Studio assets include a standard license for personal and individual commercial use. For agency-wide distribution, please contact our support team.
