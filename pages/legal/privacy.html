Privacy Standard

Trust is the currency of this Universe. We reject data clutter just as we reject digital filler.

Last Updated: January 2026

1. Data Minimalism

At Kynar Universe, we operate on a principle of necessity. We do not collect personal data unless it is strictly required to deliver your digital upgrades. We do not sell, trade, or broadcast your information to third parties.

2. Your Inventory

To maintain your centralized Inventory, we record purchase history and the email address associated with your account. This ensures you can always access your production-ready scripts and tools.

3. Local Environment

We use local storage solely to remember your Visual Atmosphere preference. This data stays on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

Data Rights

You own your digital footprint. To request a copy of your history or a full deletion of your record, contact our compliance team.
