Privacy Protocol

Last Updated: System Time Current

1. Data Minimization

Kynar Universe adheres to a strict "No Filler" data policy. We only collect what is mechanically necessary to process your transaction (Email, Payment Metadata via Stripe).

2. The Vault (Inventory)

Your purchase history is stored securely via Supabase Authentication. We do not sell your purchase history to third-party data brokers.

3. Cookies

We use local storage ("The Vault") to save your theme preferences and shopping cart. These are strictly functional.
