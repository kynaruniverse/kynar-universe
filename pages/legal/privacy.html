Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

At Kynar Universe, we believe in digital clarity. This extends to how we handle your data.

1. What we collect

When you purchase a tool or create an account, we collect only what is necessary to deliver your product: your email address and transaction history. We do not sell your data.

2. Cookies

We use local storage to save your visual preferences (Dark/Light mode). These stay on your device and are not tracked by us.

3. Contact

For data requests, please email privacy@kynaruniverse.co.uk.
